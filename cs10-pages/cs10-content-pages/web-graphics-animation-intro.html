Animate Sunrise Assignment

Animate your sunrise drawing so that the following occurs:

  • one cloud moves left off of the screen.
  • the other cloud moves right off of the screen.
  • the sun rises up in the sky.
  • the sun gets larger in size.
  • the sun changes from red to yellow.
